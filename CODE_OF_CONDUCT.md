# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration. Contributors should:

- communicate with clarity and empathy
- assume good intent
- give technical feedback without personal attacks
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or intimidation

## Unacceptable Behavior

- abusive or insulting language
- discriminatory comments
- deliberate disruption of collaboration
- publication of private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict access
for behavior that harms the project or its contributors.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the project maintainer through the repository owner.
